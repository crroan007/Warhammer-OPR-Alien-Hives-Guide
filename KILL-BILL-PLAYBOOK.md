# KILL BILL — Prime Brothers vs Orc Marauders

**Game:** One Page Rules — Grimdark Future, Core Rules **v3.5.1**
**Points:** 1000 | **Your army:** Prime Brothers | **Bill:** Orc Marauders (Feb-2026 book)
**Your job:** survive the charge on superior armor, shoot his fragile killers off the board, and **hold more objective markers than him at the end of Round 4.** You do NOT need to table him.

---

## 0. CONFIRM ON GAME DAY (tap the "ⓘ" tooltips in Army Forge)

1. **Force Org rules?** Ask Bill if you're using the optional Force Org (max 5 units, max 2 heroes, no unit >350pts, max 2 copies). The list below is legal either way. If **no** Force Org, see the swap note in §1.

3. **"Ferocious"** (Orc army rule) — exact wording. Treat it as **Furious** (a natural 6 to-hit *on the charge* = 1 extra hit) until you read the tooltip.
4. **What Bill's big belt-fed-gun orc model "counts as."** It's cosmetic — only the datasheet matters. Likely his Warlord/Hero (possibly with a ranged weapon). Ask; don't assume.

## 2. RULES CRASH COURSE (everything you need, in order)

### The dice
- **One six-sided die for everything.** Roll **≥ the number** to succeed. **6 always succeeds, 1 always fails**, no matter the modifiers.
- **To HIT** = roll ≥ your **Quality**. You're Q3 → hit on **3+ (67%)**. Orcs are Q4 in melee (50%) but **Q5 when shooting** (their "Bad Shot" rule — their guns are bad, 33%).
- **To SAVE** = the *defender* rolls ≥ their **Defense**. You're D3 → save on **3+**. **AP(X) subtracts X from the save roll.** So vs AP2 you save on 5+, vs AP4 you save only on a natural 6.
- Sequence: attacker rolls hits → defender rolls a save per hit → each *failed* save = 1 wound → remove a model (or reduce a Tough model's wounds).

### A turn (the game is 4 rounds)
- **Alternating activation:** you activate **one unit**, then Bill activates one, back and forth until all units have gone. Then the round ends. **Whoever finishes a round first goes first next round.**
- When you activate a unit, pick **one action:**

| Action | Move | Can shoot? |
|---|---|---|
| **Hold** | 0" | Yes |
| **Advance** | 6" | Yes (after moving) |
| **Rush** | 12" | No |
| **Charge** | 12" | No — moves into melee |

- Models in a unit stay within 1" of a buddy and 9" of the whole unit.

### Melee (this is how Bill wins, so know it cold)
1. Charger moves up to 12" into base contact and **strikes first** (rolls its melee attacks like shooting).
2. Defender then **may strike back** (optional).
3. Compare total **wounds dealt** by each side. The side that dealt **fewer** wounds **loses the combat** and must take a **morale test**.
4. **Fatigue:** after a unit has charged **or** struck back, it only hits on **natural 6s in melee for the rest of that round.** ← *This is the whole counter-punch engine. A unit that already fought is weak if it fights again that round.*

### Morale, Shaken, Rout
- A unit tests morale (roll ≥ Quality) when, at the end of an activation, it's at **≤50% of its starting size** (or its Tough value for single models), **or** it lost a melee.
- **Fail → Shaken:** the unit sits idle next activation, auto-fails further morale, **and cannot hold or contest objectives.** It must spend one full activation doing nothing to recover.
- **Lost a melee while already at ≤50% and failed the test → ROUTED** (removed from play). *This is how you make whole Orc units vanish without killing every model.*
- You are **Fearless** — much harder to break.

### Winning: it's an OBJECTIVE game
- Place **D3+2** markers (3-5). You alternate placing them outside deployment zones, >9" apart.
- **Seize a marker:** be **within 3" of it with no enemy within 3"** at the end of a round. It stays yours after you leave — until an enemy contests it (then it's neutral).
- **A Shaken unit can't hold or contest.**
- **Most markers controlled at the end of Round 4 wins.** You can lose every model and still win on markers, or kill nothing and win.

### Terrain
- **Cover:** +1 to your Defense saves vs shooting (does NOT help vs Blast).
- **Difficult terrain:** capped at 6" move.

### Special rules you'll actually use
- **Blast(X):** ignores cover; each hit becomes **X hits, up to the number of models in the target unit.** Your Turret (Blast3) into a 10-orc mob = up to 6 hits from 2 shots. Anti-horde gold.
- **AP(X):** -X to enemy saves (above).
- **Deadly(X):** each wound is multiplied by X **against one model** — for killing big Tough things (Bill's Walker). (You only have this if you swap in Eradication.)
- **Reliable:** the weapon hits on **2+** regardless of Quality (your flamers basically always hit).
- **Relentless:** shooting at targets >9" away, natural 6s to hit = +1 hit (Blaster/Infernal).
- **Indirect / Artillery:** Turret shoots without line of sight, ignores cover, +1 to hit vs >9", and is -2 to be shot from >9". Keep it hidden and safe.
- **Furious** (Bill): natural 6 to hit **on the charge** = +1 hit. Why his charge is scarier than his stand-still melee.
- **Tough(X):** model takes X wounds before dying. Your Aggro/Blaster... (Aggro/Prime-elite are Tough3; the Turret is Tough3).
- **Ambush / Scout / Flying:** Bill may have units that deploy late/forward or move over things. Don't leave your Turret or backfield naked.

## 3. KNOW YOUR ENEMY — Bill's Orcs

**Army-wide:** **Bad Shot** = all Orc shooting hits only on **5+** (their guns are a non-threat at range). They win by **reaching melee** and **out-bodying you on objectives.**

### Threat tiers — what to kill and in what order

**TIER 1 — fragile killers (shoot these FIRST; they die to anything):**
- **Berserkers** — 5× Q4 **Defense 6 (!)**, Berserker Claws **A1 AP4**. AP4 ignores your armor in melee, but **D6 means they fail ~83% of saves vs *any* shooting.** Delete them before they charge.
- **Berserker Beast Riders** — 3× Q4 **D6** Tough3, fast cavalry, Claws **AP4** + Jaws AP1. Same deal: hit hard, die to shooting, and they're *fast* — prioritize.

**TIER 2 — durable killer (needs focused AP/melee, NOT small arms):**
- **Melee Walker** — Q4 **D2 Tough12**, 2× Walker Saw **A2 AP2 Deadly3** + Stomp, Fear2. Its saws can chunk a brick (Deadly3). It is **not** fragile — kill it with **massed AP4** (Aggro's 12 fists + Blaster plasma) or avoid feeding it an isolated unit.

**TIER 3 — delivery & bodies:**
- **Transport Truck (190) / Battle Truck (385):** Fast, **Impact**, carry melee mobs ("bumping trucks"). **Kill the truck before the passengers get out** — strand them. Impact hits are AP0 (your armor eats them).
- **Orc Warriors / Orc Mob:** 10× Q4 **D5**, mostly **AP0** melee. **These bounce off your Defense 3** — they're an objective/body threat, not a killing threat. But respect volume: 20 attacks + Furious 6s will still chip you over time, so don't let a mob freely grind a brick for 3 rounds.
- **Orc Bikers:** Q4 D5 Tough3, Fast — objective grabbers. Plasma them.

## 4. THE PLAN — round by round

### Deployment
- Deploy within 12" of your long edge. **Castle toward the 2 objectives nearest you**; you don't need the far ones early.
- **Support Turret:** hide it out of line of sight (it's Indirect — it doesn't need to see). Keep it >9" from likely chargers.
- **Aggro + Blaster** central, mutually supporting. **Prime Brothers on your safe objective.** **Infernal Brothers screen 3-4" in front** of Aggro/your guns so nothing can reach them turn 1.
- **Keep your back units ~13-14" from Bill's front** so his 12" charges can't reach turn 1.
- **Deploy your expensive units LAST if you can** — see where his Walker/trucks go and angle accordingly.
- **Activation tip:** each round, activate your **cheap units first** (Infernal, Prime Brothers) and your **beatstick last**, so you commit after seeing Bill's move.

### Round 1 — set up, don't overcommit
- **Hold / small Advance** for firing lanes. Do not walk into his charge range.
- **Shooting priority:** Berserkers / Beast Riders **on sight** (Blaster plasma + Turret) — they evaporate. If a Truck is rushing up, start shooting **the truck** (Blaster AP4 vs D2).
- Walk Prime Brothers onto your objective.

### Round 2 — the absorb & the truck
- If a **Truck** comes in, **focus it** (Blaster + Turret) to pop it before the mob disembarks. If the mob spills out bunched up, that's a **Blast feast** — Turret + Infernal flamers shred it.
- Let his mobs charge your **screen / a brick.** Their AP0 mostly bounces (you save on 3+). You'll lose little; he commits.
- Keep killing Tier-1 fragile units before they reach you.

### Round 3 — counter-punch (the kill move)
- **The Fatigue trick:** a mob that charged you last activation has **already fought** — if it's still locked or you re-engage same round, it hits back on **6s only.** So:
  - When you got charged, you survived (AP0 vs D3). On **your** turn, **charge that same unit with Aggro** → your 12 AP4 attacks hit full, their reply is fatigued → you win the combat → they take morale → if they're at ≤50% they **rout.**
- Aggro's target priority in melee: **Berserkers > Beast Riders > Melee Walker > mobs.**
- Throw all AP4 (Aggro fists + Blaster plasma) at the **Melee Walker** if it's close; ~9-10 wounds in a turn cripples its Tough12.

### Round 4 — STOP SHOOTING, STAND ON POINTS
- This is where games are lost by new players. **Movement beats damage now.** Move durable bodies **onto markers** (within 3", no enemy within 3").
- **Prime Brothers + Infernal + Aggro** physically occupy objectives. They're Fearless and armored — Bill's AP0 can't shift them in one turn.
- **Contest** any marker Bill holds by getting a model within 3" of it (denies it to him).
- Count markers. You only need **one more than Bill.**

### The two things that beat you — and the fix
1. **Getting alpha-charged before you're set / out-activated.** Fix: deploy back 13"+, screen with Infernal, activate cheap-first / beatstick-last, never advance into 12" range on R1.
2. **The Melee Walker isolating and Deadly3-ing a single brick.** Fix: never feed it one lone unit; bait it into a screen or gang it with Aggro + Blaster. Don't chase it across the board — it's one model and can't win the objective game alone.

## 5. CHEAT SHEET

**Your to-hit:** 3+ (67%). **Reliable weapons** (your flamers): 2+.
**Your save (D3):** vs AP0 = 3+ · vs AP1 = 4+ · vs AP2 = 5+ · vs AP3/AP4 = 6 only.
**Orc shooting:** always 5+ (33%) — a non-threat.
**Orc melee vs your D3:** basic CCW (AP0) you save 3+ (bounces) · Berserker AP4 you save on 6 only · Walker saw AP2+Deadly3 you save 5+, each wound ×3.

**Your shooting vs Orcs:**
- vs **D6 Berserkers/Beast Riders:** anything works (they save on 6 only). **Plasma/Turret them turn 1.**
- vs **D5 mobs:** use **volume + Blast** (Turret, flamers), NOT plasma (overkill/waste).
- vs **D2 Tough12 Walker / D2 trucks:** **AP4** (plasma + fists) and focus fire.

**Priority order:** Berserkers/Beast Riders → trucks (before they unload) → Melee Walker → bikers → mobs (last; just screen/Blast them off objectives).

**Golden rules:**
1. Make the Orcs charge YOU; survive the AP0; counter-punch with AP4 next.
2. Blast the hordes, plasma/fists the elites and the Walker.
3. Round 4 = stand on objectives, not shoot.
4. Activate cheap units first, beatstick last.
5. You win on **markers**, not kills.
