# KILL BILL v2 — Alien Hives vs Human Defense Force

**Game:** One Page Rules — Grimdark Future, Core Rules **v3.5.1** | **1000pts**
**You:** Alien Hives (monster-heavy) | **Bill:** Human Defense Force (gunline + artillery + tanks)
**Why this army:** HDF's whole offense is **Blast weapons + low-accuracy volume fire**. A single big monster is a **1-model unit, so any Blast weapon does at most 1 hit to it** — their flagship cannons are neutered. Meanwhile your beasts cross the board through the fire (Fast/Tough/Fearless) and **crush them in the melee HDF cannot fight.** You win on **objective markers**, not kills.

## 2. THE RULES THAT MATTER FOR THIS ARMY

- **Blast(X) is capped at the number of models in the target unit.** Your single-model beasts (Carnivo-Rex) cap ALL Blast at **1 hit**. Your 3–5 model units cap it at 3–5. *This is the core reason you beat a Blast army — keep your beasts as single-model units, never "screen" them into a blob.*
- **Tough(X):** a model takes X wounds before dying; Tough12 means a Rex eats 12 wounds. Spread incoming fire is wasted on it.
- **Regeneration** (Venom/Toxico if taken): roll a die per wound, **5+ ignores it.**
- **Fast:** +2" Advance, **+4" Rush/Charge** → your beasts Rush 16" and charge 16". Soul-Snatchers/Ravenous/Snatcher Lord cross fast. **Strider:** ignore difficult terrain.
- **Rending** (Soul-Snatchers, Snatcher Lord): natural 6 to hit = that hit gets **AP5** (and ignores Regeneration). Lets light claws hurt Defense-2 tanks.
- **AP1** (most of your claws): vs HDF Defense-5 infantry → they save on 6+ (you wreck them). Vs Defense-2 vehicles → they still save on 3+ (so you need *volume* + Rending to grind tanks — gang up).
- **Melee:** your beast charges, **strikes first** (13 attacks!), HDF strikes back feebly (Q5, A1). **Fatigue:** after your beast fights, it only hits on 6s the rest of that round — so don't expect a second full melee in one round.
- **Fear(X)** on beasts: counts as +X extra wounds when deciding who won a melee — helps win combats.
- **Hold the Line (Bill):** his units don't flee. Killing half a squad won't rout it. **Win by contesting/holding markers, not by breaking him.**

## 3. KNOW YOUR ENEMY — Bill's HDF

**Army-wide:** poor shooting (most units **Quality 5–6**, mostly **AP0**), fragile bodies (**Defense 5** infantry, **Defense 2** vehicles), terrible in melee (Q5, A1). **"Hold the Line" = no morale routs.** Wins by sitting on objectives and shooting.

### What to kill, and how

**TIER 1 — the support web (race your fast units here and assassinate it):** These are fragile and they make the gunline dangerous.
- **Field Artillery / Weapon Teams** (D2 Tough3 / D5 Tough3) — Blast3 **Indirect** (ignore your cover & line of sight). Weapon Teams can upgrade to **Laser Cannon (AP3 Deadly3)** — the gun that actually threatens your beasts. Kill these first.
- **Support Vehicle** (255, D2 Tough6) — Indirect rockets + buffs.
- **Snipers** (Takedown) — pick off your Snatcher Lord / lone models. Don't leave the hero exposed.

**TIER 2 — the big guns (your beasts tank these; gang them in melee):**
- **Battle Tank** (D2 Tough12, Nova Cannon **Blast6**) — Blast6 does **1 hit** to a Rex. Walk up and eat it in melee.
- **Heavy Battle Tank** (D2 Tough15) — **Fusion Array AP4 Deadly3** is one of the few guns that hurts your beasts (~2.5 dmg/turn). Still survives 4+ turns.
- **Super Heavy** (890, D2 Tough24, **AP4 Blast6 Indirect**) — Force-Org-illegal. If it appears (no FO), ignore it where possible and win objectives elsewhere; it's one model and can't hold the board.
- **Walkers** (Tough12–18, D2) — gang with beasts.
- **Gunships** (Aircraft, Laser Cannon AP3 Deadly3) — can't hold objectives; tank the shots, win on markers.

### Quick math that wins you the game
- **Nova Cannon Blast6 → Carnivo-Rex: 1 hit.** Their best horde gun is useless on your beasts.
- **Carnivo-Rex charges a 10-man Infantry Squad:** 13 attacks → ~6–7 hits, AP1 vs D5 → **5+ guardsmen dead in one charge.**
- **HDF Fusion Array (their scariest gun) → Rex:** ~2.5 damage/turn. A Tough12 Rex shrugs the gunline for 4+ turns — long enough to reach melee and win.

## 4. THE PLAN — round by round

### Deployment
- HDF will castle a gunline with screens in front of artillery/tanks. You deploy within 12" of your edge.
- **Two Carnivo-Rex front and center** as the unkillable spearhead — they soak the fire so your fragile fast units don't.
- **Soul-Snatchers + Ravenous Beasts on the flanks**, slightly behind the beasts / using terrain, ready to sprint.
- **Snatcher Lord** wherever it can run to a flank objective safely (or join Ravenous Beasts).
- **Try to win first turn** (or be happy going first) — every turn you're not advancing is a turn of free shooting for Bill.

### Round 1 — advance into the teeth, soak the fire
- **Rush the beasts forward 16"** straight up the middle. They're the lightning rod — let them eat the Blast (which does ~1 each) and the AP-Deadly (which does ~2–3 each; they have 12 wounds).
- **Advance/Rush the fast units behind cover** toward the flanks. Keep them out of line of sight where possible — they're your scorers and they're fragile.
- Don't worry about his shooting hitting the beasts. That's the trade you want.

### Round 2 — make contact
- **Charge the beasts into his line** — priority: whatever's nearest that you can reach. A Rex into an Infantry Squad deletes it; a Rex into a tank starts grinding it (gang if you can).
- **Sprint the fast units (Soul-Snatchers Strider, Ravenous Fast, Snatcher Lord) toward his TIER-1 support** — artillery, weapon teams, support vehicle. Soul-Snatchers' Rending claws shred them.
- Beasts are now in melee = safe from most of his shooting (he risks hitting his own in combat / they're locked).

### Round 3 — assassinate the support, grab markers
- **Kill the artillery/weapon teams/support vehicle** with your fast units — this turns off his Indirect fire and buffs.
- **Beasts keep grinding** the tanks/walkers (Rending + volume). Even a Tough15 tank falls to a Rex + Soul-Snatchers ganging it.
- **Start positioning for objectives** — get fast bodies within 3" of markers.

### Round 4 — STAND ON / CONTEST MARKERS
- **This wins the game.** Movement > damage now. Get a model within 3" of every marker you can, and **within 3" of his markers to contest them** (one model denies him the point).
- His blobs won't rout (Hold the Line), so where you can't clear them, **contest** to neutralize rather than trying to kill everything.
- **Count markers — you need one more than Bill.**

### Your three risks & fixes
1. **You get out-activated / out-bodied on objectives** (5 units vs his many). Fix: propose Force Org (caps him at 5); hold your fast units to activate LATE and contest markers after he commits; use the beasts' durability to commit first without worry.
2. **His few AP-Deadly guns (Fusion Array, Laser Cannons) focus a beast.** Fix: two beasts split the fire; consider the Venom/Toxico Regen swap; they still survive 4+ turns — just reach melee.
3. **Snipers kill your Snatcher Lord.** Fix: keep the hero behind the beasts / in a unit until you sprint it to an objective late.

## 5. CHEAT SHEET

**Your to-hit:** Q3 units (Soul-Snatchers, Snatcher Lord) hit on **3+**; Q4 beasts/Ravenous on **4+**.
**Blast vs your beasts:** 1 model = **max 1 hit**. (Never blob your beasts.)
**Your claws (AP1) vs HDF:** D5 infantry save 6+ (you wreck them) · D2 vehicles save 3+ (need volume + Rending — gang up).
**HDF shooting:** Q5–6, mostly AP0 → low threat. Their only beast-hurters: Fusion Array (AP4 Deadly3), Laser Cannons (AP3 Deadly3) — there aren't many.

**Target priority:** Tier-1 support (artillery, weapon teams, support vehicle, snipers) with fast units → infantry squads on objectives → tanks/walkers (gang in melee) → ignore the Super Heavy/aircraft if you can win markers without them.

**Golden rules:**
1. Beasts go up the middle and EAT the gunline — that's their job, let them get shot.
2. Single-model beasts make Blast do 1 hit — never screen them into a blob.
3. Fast units flank, kill the artillery/support, then grab markers.
4. Round 4 = stand on / contest objectives. Contest (don't try to kill) the no-rout blobs.
5. You win on **markers**, not kills. Propose Force Org to cap his numbers.
