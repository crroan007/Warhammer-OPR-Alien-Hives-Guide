- Create or log into a **MyMiniFactory** account using the **exact same email you used to sign up for Patreon**.
- OPR shares each month's models into that MMF account automatically. You'll get a **redemption/share email** — this can take **up to ~2 business days** after subscribing.
- **Why:** the STL models are NOT on Patreon as files — they're delivered to your MMF **library**. Wrong email = models don't show up.

### Step 3 — Download the STLs from your MMF library

- You should see this month's **Collector** release, the **Throwback** set, **bases/terrain**, and the **Welcome Pack**.
- Download each collection (MMF gives per-object or whole-collection **ZIP** downloads). Save them somewhere with room — this is **several GB**.
- **Why:** this is items 1–4. These stay in your MMF library **forever, even after you cancel** — but only once they've actually appeared here.

### Step 4 — Grab the digital extras from the Patreon post
- Back in the current month's Patreon reward post, download the **short stories, bonus missions, paper miniatures, and artwork** attachments/links.
- **Why:** item 5. These are post attachments, not on MMF.

### Step 5 — Save your 50% discount code (optional back-catalog)

- **Why:** item 6. This is the only way to get older releases, and the discount only applies while you're a patron.

### Step 6 — (Optional) Free paper minis on DriveThruRPG
