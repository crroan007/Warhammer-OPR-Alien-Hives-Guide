🎲 NPC Narrator

Grimdark Future · v3.5 Solo & Co-Op AI · runs entirely on your device · ← Full battle guide

Runs the AI opponent's turn and narrates it aloud. Set the AI army once (it saves on this device), then tap Next AI Activation each AI turn — it rolls the section, picks a random unit, walks its decision tree (you answer a board question or two it can't see, by tap or voice), and tells you exactly what the AI does.

AI Army

Type sets the decision tree. Flags change behaviour (Artillery, Aircraft, Caster, Ambush, Scout, Counter, Fast). Tick Shaken during play — Shaken units activate last and just recover.
